"Girl With A Pearl Earring"

"Girl With A Pearl Earring" is an oil painting by Johannes Vermeer. Going by various names over the centuries, it became known by its present title towards the end of the 20th century after the large pearl earring worn by the girl portrayed there. In 2006, the Dutch public selected it as the most beautiful painting in the Netherlands.

"The Kiss"

1907
The Kiss is the most acclaimed work of Gustav Klimpt of two lifesized figures in a passionate embrace draped in gold. Considered to represent the apex of his "Golden Period", the painting takes influence from Byzantine works.

"Women in the Garden"

1866
An Early masterpiece of Claude Monet started when he was just 26 years old. His wife and friends posed for the scene in a garden Monet rented for the occasion. The dresses themselves were detailed after the initial staging of the scene based on the contents of a popular fashion magazine.

"Portrait of Fritza Riedler"

1906
A painting embellished with gold and silver leaf, the geometric design of this work pulls the viewer away from her body and the armchair and brings focus to her face. This is accomplished with the semicircular motif lying behind her head. The motif is thought of to have been inspired by Egyptian art and the works of Diego Velazquez.

"Love Letter"

"Love Letter" is an oil painting by Johannes Vermeer. In 1971 the canvas was stolen by cutting it off the frame: the result was the loss of about half a centimeter of painted surface on each side. It is the only painting in which Vermeer constructs the scene beyond a door.

"The Milkmaid"

"The Milkmaid" is an oil painting by Johannes Vermeer. The exact year of the painting's completion is unknown, with estimates varying by source. The painting is strikingly illusionistic, conveying not just details but a sense of the weight of the woman and the table.

"Woman Reading a Letter"

"Woman Reading a Letter" is an oil painting by Johannes Vermeer made around 1663. The painting is unique among Vermeer's interiors in that no fragment of corner, floor or ceiling can be seen.
